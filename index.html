SenseWatch Technology

We provide the tools for smartwatch users and developers to simplify the process of creating powerful sensor-based smartwatch applications

Gesture Recognition

SenseWatch can recognize your gesture from the acclerometer and gyroscope on your smartwatch, and allows you to define your own gestures as a shortcut to your own tasks!

Vital Sign Interpretation

SenseWatch also reads your vital sign from your smartwatch, including heartrate, skin tempreature and perspiration, allowing you to interact with your environment in a whole new way.

Uber Usecase

"U" is for Uber

Say you are going home from a business meeting with your hands full, and you would like to request an uber car. Instead of reaching for your phone in your pocket, unlock it, go to the Uber app to set up a pickup, you can simply draw a big U shape in the air, and then your uber will be on the way to your location.

Smart Home Control

Home in your control, with SenseWatch

The app controls room temperature and lighting color and intensity based on vital sign, such as user's mood and status of sleeping/awaking inferred from heart rate and skin temperature. We implement this feature using Philips Hue smart lighting and Nest Smart Thermostat that are connected to the smartwatch using our API.
